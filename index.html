About Us

Team 5327A 2014-2015
From Left to Right: Gagan, Chris, Koji, Billy, Neetika, Kush, Navneedh, Ray

We are a VEX Robotics team from Dublin, California.

map-embed.com

The 5327A VEX Robotics Teams has had a rich history. Founded at the creation of our robotics club, this team has been the cornerstone of our club. For the last two years this team has gone to both the World and the State Championships and has won a plethora of awards. This year, our team consists of 8 members, 1 senior, 3 juniors, 2 sophomores, and 2 freshmen. With a diverse group of curious and intelligent students we strive to teach each other and work together to solve our problems. We can't wait to start this design process and have a great season!
